Proxmox Homelab

under active development

Built my own homelab from scratch – as a practical learning environment for everything relevant to my job and further training. A Proxmox server with VMs and LXC containers, a segmented network with VLANs and firewall zones, a self-hosted DNS filter and publicly reachable services without any open ports.

learned: virtualization, resource planning, network segmentation, service separation and secure operation of self-hosted services.

  • host: proxmox ve · debian
  • net: vlans · firewall zones
  • dns: pi-hole · dns filter
  • public: cloudflare tunnel · no open ports

proxmox-ve · debian · lxc · vms · vlans · dns/pi-hole · cloudflare-tunnel
